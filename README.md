# Age Calculator

A simple, interactive web-based age calculator that determines your exact age in years, months, and days.

## Features

- 🎂 **Calculate Age**: Enter your birth date to instantly calculate your age
- 📅 **Detailed Information**: Shows years, months, days, and total days lived
- ✅ **Input Validation**: Prevents future dates and invalid inputs
- 📱 **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile devices
- 🎨 **Modern UI**: Clean, gradient-based interface with smooth animations

## How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Select your birth date from the date picker
3. Click "Calculate Age" or press Enter
4. View your age with detailed breakdown

## Files

- `index.html` - Main HTML structure
- `style.css` - Styling and responsive design
- `script.js` - Age calculation logic

## Features in Detail

- **Age Display**: Shows your current age in years
- **Full Breakdown**: Complete age in years, months, and days
- **Birth Information**: Displays your birth date in a readable format
- **Total Days**: Shows the total number of days you've lived
- **Validation**: Ensures birth date is not in the future

## Browser Support

Works in all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
